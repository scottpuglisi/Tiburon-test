Why don't you have keypads?

Keypads may seem like a great addition at first, but upon closer examination you can see the drawbacks. Our main locker philosophy is to give you the correct ratio of payment points to lockers. This is because your guests spend exponentially more time interacting with a kiosk when renting a locker, not when accessing it. Having less payment points only saves companies from having to spend more on them. ((Keypads shouldn’t be a substitution for payment points.)) Keypads also create another point of failure. They're not waterproof, and also subject to excessive grime, rust, mold, and bacteria because of all the unsealed crevices. **one draw back is to effective kill bacteria and viruses, it’s very difficult to do this on an electronic keypad in this day and age, and much easier on a kiosk. Hit harder!
