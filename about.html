How We Started

It was 2004 and Jared Lowenthal sat on a beach in Mexico, forced to sit and watch his family's valuables while watching them cool off in the ocean. His hotel room was a 20 minute walk each way from the beach, so Jared asked the hotel personnel where the pay lockers were. To his frustration, they laughed at him, but it was in that moment Jared decided to start the ultimate rental locker company - Tiburon Lockers.

Jared used his extensive experience in real estate and property management, combined with his creative and entrepreneurial spirit, to start Tiburon Lockers. To help make his idea of safe, secure, and simple storage at beaches and beyond a reality, he recruited a group of seasoned business veterans, technology professionals, and engineers to carry out his vision.

To build an unbeatable electronic locker, Jared knew that the systems must be developed, owned, and supported by an in-house staff of engineers. It was the only way to create a locker of the highest quality and fully control Tiburon's destiny. The newly assembled team used their experience and drive to create the perfect locker, making Tiburon dynamic international company with operations in the US, Canada, and beyond.
