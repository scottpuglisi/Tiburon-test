Tiburon's Pro-Tech Lockers

Front line defense against viruses to keep you safe

Safety has always been a vitally important part of Tiburon’s mission. We listen to our customers and are always ready to adapt and develop new products to fit needs that are constantly changing.

Now, more than ever, it is vital to our communal health and safety that we all take the necessary precautions to prevent the spread of bacteria and viruses. It's of the upmost importance that we promote the highest level of sanitation in public spaces.

Tiburon's COVID-19 response has been swift and comprehensive; we endeavor to revolutionize clean lockers and kiosks by integrating the latest innovations in health technology. We have:

New state of the art lockers that are coated with a cutting-edge antimicrobial paint coating, adding an extra layer of protection.

Platinum Kiosks that use intelligent, automated UV-C light disinfecting treatments. These systems continuously monitors to detect when surfaces have been used and safely treats the surfaces hundreds of times a day, without any assistance or any staff interaction.

Technology is more than 99% effective against bacteria, fungi, and viruses “Touchless” kiosk access through our rental app (coming this fall)
