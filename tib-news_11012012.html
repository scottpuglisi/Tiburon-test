Tiburon Lockers Inc. Announces Release Date for New Rapid Rental System

November 1, 2012

ROCKLEIGH, NJ - Tiburon Lockers will release the Rapid Access stations in the spring of 2013. The system will be the fastest means available for guests to access their locker. After renting a locker at the kiosk, guests will no longer have the need to return to the kiosk, thus avoiding queuing. This will also make the system flexible for a variety of new technologies as part of Tiburon's focus on enhancing the guest and operator experiences.

Tiburon Lockers - a turnkey locker storage solution with unique in-house engineering. More than 400 venues have experienced our passion for servicing, as evidenced by exclusive live monitoring technology and real-time customer support. Tiburon Lockers continues to provide unmatched service with a focus on developing useful innovations in secure storage solutions for ski resorts, amusement parks, water parks, secure government institutions as well as many other venue types.
